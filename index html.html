Chocolate cake

Hello everyone,in this site you will learn how to make your favorite chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 small spoon backing powder
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 small spoon vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Mix dry ingredients (sugar,floor,cocoa powder...)
  3. Mix wet ingredients (oil,milk,egg...)
  4. Combine everything
  5. Put batter in a small pan
  6. Bake the mixture for 20-25 minutes
  7. Enjoy your cake
